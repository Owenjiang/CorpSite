Meaning Based Security & Surveillance – One Package For All

The Need

Security and surveillance operations have become an increasingly important consideration at a corporate, national and global level as organizations strive to protect individuals and mitigate against risks posed to public and corporate infrastructure assets. While security and surveillance operations form an integral part of any security strategy, the ability to understand, analyze and interpret the significance of information gathered as part of surveillance operations is a fundamental requirement for all security environments today

The Software Era

Increasing volumes and types of data, including video footage, camera stills, vehicle registration plates, traffic records, police databases, immigration records, telephone conversations and transaction reports form part of security operations and point to the need to adopt comprehensive strategies which encompass all security data and where relevant, integrate these with operational data as well. The Security & Surveillance society is evolving from pure hardware centric, HD Cameras/Adapters/Workstations, into a software era. When infrastructure is ready and network bandwidth allows huge video streaming to be transmitted to control center in real time, it is time to do some real analysis against massive video data for a community, a city even a country. We are seeing more and ore hardware centric service provider is losing the edge versus enterprise software service vendor stands up and facing the challenge.

The Technology

Autonomy Virage’s security and surveillance products and solutions support every stage of security and surveillance operations, improve and promote situational awareness, provide an extensive range of advanced recognition and recording systems as well as capabilities for instant and post-event retrieval and analytics.

A flexible and entirely modular architecture allows customers to select products according to their specific requirements to create tailor-made, highly sophisticated security systems that can evolve as requirements change. All products may be used either as individual components on a standalone basis or multi-linked to each other to create a centralized control and management center.

Combining world leading technologies from security, infrastructure and video domain expertise, Autonomy Virage offers one of the most sophisticated security and surveillance solutions in the world. Pioneering advanced recognition technologies originally developed for the security services lie at the heart of Autonomy Virage and provide a strong foundation for a sophisticated product range which includes Digital/Network Video Recording (DVR/NVR), Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR), Intelligent Scene Analysis System (iSAS), Electronic Point of Sale Monitoring (EPOS), Container Surveillance and Management System (CSM), face recognition and audio analysis.

The Core

Autonomy’s unique technology allows organizations to monitor vast quantities of information from disparate sources, understand all data gathered at a conceptual level and place each piece of information in context with other known intelligence. At the heart of Autonomy’s groundbreaking technology is the Intelligent Data Operating Layer (IDOL), a single platform which is optimized for fast processing and retrieval of data in all formats. By automatically understanding the inherent meaning and potential significance of any piece of information, Autonomy’s technology brings fresh, intelligent insight and immediate analytical capabilities to all security environments.

Capabilities include:

  • Advanced Digital and Network Video Management Solutions
  • Intelligent scene analysis with object tracking
  • Counting and behavioural analysis
  • Video forensic analysis
  • Number or License plate recognition
  • Audio recognition
  • Container surveillance and management
  • Advanced conceptual analytics
  • Electronic point of sale monitoring
