Legal Document & Records Management

To stay competitive while managing the pressures of increased risk and the inevitable impact on productivity and client service, law firms, legal department of enterprise or professional services firm, require an intelligent, comprehensive and automated approach to organize, find and govern all content across the firm. Lawyers or professionals want solutions that enable them to perform at the highest levels of efficiency and effectiveness, with a user experience tailored to their specific working practices. These firms or departments need technology that compels user adoption as a means to influence users to comply with important firm policy. To ensure user acceptance, the policies necessary to protect the firm must also be easy for the individual to adopt.

Legacy approaches and solutions for managing content have become insufficient for today's data volumes, user expectations and global workforces. Autonomy uses the advanced technology of IDOL to deliver a solution that provides a quantum leap in productivity for law firms and professional services firms. Autonomy holds the key to complete information and risk management solutions for these professionals.

Product Features:

  • Matter Centric View - Organize, capture and maintain all matter related documents, emails and other materials
  • Robust Document Management - Manage documents, case files, and email to ensure that ethical walls are maintained as well as a comprehensive audit history for regulatory, legal or operational purposes
  • Information Access - The ability to locate any content in the organization across languages and formats; including audio, video, web content, email and IM
  • Expertise Location - Build communities of expertise to quickly and easily locate subject matter or industry experts by automatically clustering content contributors and resources into related groups
  • Best Practice Management - Enterprise Knowledge Management that enables lawyers to add personal tags and create individual best practice document collections
  • Workflow and Automation - Intelligent document review routing and approval tracking as well as meaning-based automation of manual tasks
  • Contract Management - Management of clauses, templates and generation of complex documents routed for review workflow based on contract expertise

Product Differentiators:

  • Understanding Meaning - Built on unique technology that drives automation by understanding the meaning of information; enabling intelligent routing and activity generation
  • End-to-End Platform - Single unified approach for document and process management with seamless connectivity across all phases of the EDRM and governance solutions
  • Unparalleled Information Access - The ability to connect to more than 400 repositories and support more than 1,000 file types, independent of language
  • Seamless End-to-End Platform - Only platform that covers entire spectrum of eDiscovery, proactive Meaning Based Governance and information management offerings on a common platform
  • Deployment Flexibility - Choice to deploy in-house, outsourced or as a hybrid combination
  • Massive Scalability - Most scalable solution with over 14 petabytes and 30 billion messages stored, 6 billion pages in active litigation review and 3 million files processed per hour
  • Proven Successful - Used today by 10 of the top 10 global law firms, 75% of the Global 100 law firms and 75% of the AmLaw 100
